About

The studio.

Operating Model

studio AI2K is a one-person product studio that delivers like a full development team. The difference isn't just AI-assisted code — it's that the builder understands business processes, writes the specs, architects the solution, and ships the product. No handoffs between a product manager, a designer, and a developer. One person who thinks in workflows and builds in code. That's what makes the studio fast — not shortcuts, but the elimination of translation layers between business logic and technical execution.

Stack

React, Node.js, PostgreSQL, VPS deployment. A modern full-stack architecture chosen for speed, reliability, and the ability to ship production-grade software from database to interface — in months, not years.

AI-Native Development

AI handles the heavy lifting — code generation, refactoring, testing. The builder handles product vision, architecture decisions, and quality control. The result: a complete product at a fraction of the cost and timeline of a traditional agency.
